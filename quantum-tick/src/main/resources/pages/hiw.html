How QuantumTick Works

Step 1

Setup Processes: Use Add Process to manually enter data or
Randomize to generate a set. You can also Upload a .txt
file following the format: PID, Burst, Arrival, Priority.

Step 2

Define Constraints: Ensure Burst/Arrival is 1-30 and
Priority is 1-20 with no duplicates.

Step 3

Configure Algorithm: Select your algorithm. If choosing
Round Robin, set a Quantum Time (1-10).

Step 4

Simulate: Click Simulate to generate the Gantt chart and
timing table. Use the Timer controls to watch the
execution in real-time.
